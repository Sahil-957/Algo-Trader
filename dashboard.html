    Risk Notes

    Read before going live

    This bot is built for intraday testing first. Keep PAPER_TRADE=True until you are comfortable with the flow, then switch to live trading only if your credentials and risk controls are verified.

    If the lot size looks wrong, check your .env file. The dashboard will keep showing the latest bot state even when the market is closed.
